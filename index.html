What makes Rustomjee Cliff Tower unique?

Rustomjee Cliff Tower establishes a remarkable connection between modern architecture and nature. With views of the Arabian Sea and Mount Mary Church as a cultural landmark, the residences provide exclusivity, sophistication, and an elevated lifestyle.
  • A unique community featuring 37 exclusive residences.
  • The height from one floor to the next is 10’3 inches.
  • The entrance lobby features an impressive double height of 18'
  • Each residence features a nearly private floor lobby, ensuring complete privacy for residents.
  • 4 lifts and a dedicated fire elevator serve only 2 apartments per floor to ensure maximum accessibility.
  • An expansive sundeck that is 8.5 feet deep, offering some of the city's most sought-after views.
